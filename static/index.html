About Rental Scam Detector

Protecting Australian renters from fraudulent listings and agreements.

How it works

🔍 Layer 1 — Pattern detection

28 regex rules covering payment scams (Western Union, crypto), overseas landlord claims, WhatsApp-only contact, illegal lease terms, and pressure tactics.

🧠 Layer 2 — Semantic anomaly

Sentence-transformer embeddings (all-MiniLM-L6-v2) compare each clause against 10,773 real legal clauses from 510 CUAD contracts + NSW tenancy forms.

🤖 AI Explanation

LLM generates a plain-English summary with clause-level advice. Priority: Claude AI → Mistral-7B (free) → rule-based fallback.

💬 Chatbot

Multi-turn assistant powered by the same LLM. Ask general Australian tenancy law questions or query your specific document after analysis.
